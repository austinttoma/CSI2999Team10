Module 2: Bad Practices in Recycling

Everyone thinks that when they are recycling, they are helping the environment.

However, if you are not careful you may not be making such a big impact as you think. In fact, in a government survey it was found that as much as 40% of items in the recycling bin, were not recyclable. The government survey also found that 49% of the respondents did not know that tissue paper could not be recycled.

Non-recyclable materials (this can include liquids or plastic bags) may enter the recycling stream. This can cause damage to the recycling machinery, as well as contaminate recyclable materials.
