Module 0: What really happens to your recycling?

Many of the items you place in the recycling bin, end up in landfills.

Plastic is, notably, not designed to be recycled.

After you place plastic in your recycling bin, it gets taken to materials recovery facilities. At the facility, the plastic is sorted by types (bag, bottle, etc.). The plastic, of the same type, is then squashed together in the form of a cube. It is then sent on its way to the next location.

However, shipped plastic waste rarely gets recycled when it reaches its destination. Rather than be recycled, the plastic is illegally dumped or open burning of plastic takes place.
